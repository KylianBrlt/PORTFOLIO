Smart Chicken Coop

About this project

This section details my contribution to the "Smart Chicken" year project, focusing on the automated egg collection system. As part of a larger initiative to create a smart chicken coop, my role involved designing and implementing a mechanism to automatically retrieve eggs after they were detected. This system aimed to reduce manual effort, minimize egg damage, and integrate seamlessly with the thermal egg detection. The ultimate goal was to create a fully functional smart coop, as depicted in the project overview image.

Technologies

  • Raspberry Pi (for overall system control and integration)
  • Mechanical components (motors for movement)
  • Electronics for motor control and sensing

Key Features

  • Automated Egg Retrieval: Mechanism to physically collect eggs from the nesting area after detection.
  • Automated Transfer to Storage: Directed collected eggs towards a designated storage area within the coop.
